Carpet Care Services

Dirt and soil are easily seen on a hard surface and can wipe off easily, but on carpet it can soon work its way deep into the fibers where the abrasive action will cause them to deteriorate and need replacing prematurely.

Professional Carpet Cleaning Includes:

  • Wall to Wall Carpets
  • Sanitizing / Deodorizing
  • Deep Steam Cleaning
  • Spot & Stain Removal
  • Scotchgard©
  • Stairs & Hallways
  • Shampoo Cleaning
  • Pre-Conditioning for heavily soiled areas

The Safest Cleaning Methods

Our cleaning system deep cleans your carpets and upholstery; flushing the dirt and pollutants out from the fibers with self-neutralizing cleaning chemicals that are also non-toxic and bio-degradable.

We offer only the best cleaning methods using hypo-allergenic, child- and pet-safe solutions. Serving Lehigh Valley, the Pocono Mountains, Berks County, Warren and Hunterdon counties.

Scotchgard® Protection

We can apply Scotchgard® to your carpeting, rugs, and upholstered furniture. It forms a barrier to help prevent dirt and stains from sticking to the fibers, making routine cleaning more effective and helping your surfaces stay fresh longer.
